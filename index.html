Peter Culver is a junior full-stack software developer. He is a graduate of the We Can Code It - C# Cohort. His technical skills include: C#, HTML5, CSS3, SQL, JavaScript, GitHub, React, REST API, Razor, and MVC. For 22 years, he was a K-12 public school music educator. His time as an educator provided numerous opportunities in working collaboratively with a variety of people. He has held Supervisory and Directorial roles dealing with students and colleagues, Point-Communication and Advisory roles with parents and community stakeholders, and Managerial responsibilities with various performance groups and large-scale theatrical productions. Always a lifelong learner, he is excited to travel in a new career direction and turn his love of technology into a new profession. He is looking forward to taking his working skills as an educator and applying them to his advancing technical skills working with, and eventually leading, teams of software developers.
